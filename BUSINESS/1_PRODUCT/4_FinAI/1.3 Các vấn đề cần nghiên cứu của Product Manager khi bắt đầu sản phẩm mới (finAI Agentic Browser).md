# Các vấn đề cần nghiên cứu của Product Manager khi bắt đầu sản phẩm mới (finAI Agentic Browser)

## Tổng quan: 7 Phase Research với 35 câu hỏi then chốt

Là PM bắt đầu một sản phẩm 0→1 như finAI browser, bạn cần nghiên cứu **7 phase song song**, không tuyến tính, và liên tục validate giả định. Mỗi phase có **critical questions** quyết định go/no-go.[^1][^2][^3]

***

## Phase 1: Problem-Solution Fit – "Có vấn đề thực sự không?"

### Mục tiêu

Validate rằng financial professionals thực sự có pain point đủ lớn, đủ cấp bách, và sẵn sàng trả tiền để giải quyết.[^4][^5][^1]

### Critical Questions

**1. Pain Point Discovery**

- Điểm đau số 1 của financial professionals trong workflow research hàng ngày là gì?[^1]
- Họ đang giải quyết thế nào? Trả bao nhiêu cho tools hiện tại?[^5][^1]
- Walk me through lần cuối họ gặp pain này – chi tiết từng bước.[^1]

**2. Target User Definition**

- Ai có vấn đề này nghiêm trọng nhất? (role, company size, workflow)[^2][^5]
- Một ngày của họ như thế nào? Họ dành bao nhiêu giờ cho task này?[^1]
- **Anti-persona:** Ai KHÔNG nên là target user? Ai sẽ có negative value?[^6]

**3. Value Quantification**

- Nếu solve được, user đạt outcome gì cụ thể? Tiết kiệm bao nhiêu giờ/tuần?[^5][^1]
- Opportunity cost của time họ đang waste là bao nhiêu \$\$?[^5]
- Khi nói "I'd pay X for this," X là bao nhiêu?[^7]


### Method

- **12-15 customer interviews** (pattern xuất hiện sau ~12 interviews)[^6][^1]
- 2 câu hỏi then chốt: (1) \#1 pain point, (2) real story lần cuối gặp pain[^1]
- Screen-share workflow hiện tại, đi qua từng step, highlight friction[^1]
- Job-to-be-done framework để hiểu underlying motivation[^5]


### Success Criteria

- Pattern rõ ràng trong 80%+ interviews[^6][^1]
- Users rate pain 8+/10
- Can articulate ICP trong 2 câu
- Users nói "I'd pay for that" khi nghe value prop[^5]

***

## Phase 2: Technical Feasibility – "Build được không?"

### Mục tiêu

Validate rằng AI/tech stack hiện tại CÓ THỂ deliver solution reliably, với cost hợp lý.[^8][^9][^10]

### Critical Questions

**1. AI Capability Assessment**

- LLM hiện tại (GPT-4, Claude) có thể làm task này reliably không?[^10][^11]
- Accuracy rate? Hallucination rate?[^12][^11]
- Nó pass được blind test so với human analyst không?[^11]

**2. Data Availability \& Quality**

- Có access financial data chất lượng không? (APIs, scraping, partnerships)[^13][^11]
- License cost? Rate limits? Latency?[^11]
- Estimate cost per user per month?[^11]

**3. Browser Integration Feasibility**

- Có thể extract data từ financial websites reliably không?[^14]
- Anti-bot measures sẽ block không? Legal/TOS risks?[^15][^16]
- Success rate khi test trên top 10 sites users hay dùng?

**4. Performance \& Latency**

- End-to-end task time bao lâu? User có chờ được không?[^9][^8]
- Abandonment rate nếu task >30s? >1min? >3min?
- Có thể optimize với caching, streaming, parallel execution không?

**5. Infrastructure Cost \& Unit Economics**

- LLM inference cost per workflow?[^17][^8]
- Total COGS (LLM + data + infra) per user?[^11]
- Path đến 60-70% gross margin?[^5]

**6. Security \& Privacy**

- Sensitive financial data xử lý ở đâu? Cloud hay local?[^18][^15]
- Encryption? Audit trail? Compliance với SOC2/ISO27001?[^19][^15]
- Risk của prompt injection, data leakage?[^20][^18]


### Method

- **Prompt testing:** Test GPT-4/Claude với real financial docs, measure accuracy[^10]
- **Data API testing:** Call APIs, measure latency/cost, review license terms[^11]
- **Browser automation POC:** Test Playwright trên top 10 sites, measure success rate[^14]
- **Architecture review:** Với experienced engineers, design system architecture[^11]
- **Cost modeling:** Tokens per workflow × price per token = cost per user[^11]


### Success Criteria

- AI accuracy 80%+ on core task; hallucination <5%[^11]
- Data cost model <\$20/user/mo; latency <2s[^11]
- Browser automation 90%+ success rate; no major TOS violations
- COGS <\$40/user/mo @ \$100 ARPU = 60% margin
- Security architecture approved by infosec expert[^15]

***

## Phase 3: Regulatory \& Compliance – "Hợp pháp không?"

### Mục tiêu

Validate không vi phạm regulations financial services, avoid legal landmines.[^16][^21][^15]

### Critical Questions

**1. Investment Advice Liability**

- Product có constitute "investment advice" không?[^22]
- Cần RIA (Registered Investment Advisor) license không?
- Positioning thế nào để tránh: "research tool" vs "advisor"?[^22]

**2. Data Licensing \& Redistribution**

- Được phép use/redistribute financial data không?[^19][^11]
- License restrictions từ data providers?
- Display attribution requirements?

**3. KYC/AML Requirements**

- Có trigger KYC/AML compliance không?[^16][^15]
- Là "money transmitter" theo định nghĩa regulators không?
- Nếu có, compliance cost \& timeline?[^15]

**4. Privacy Regulations (GDPR/CCPA)**

- Xử lý EU/California user data thế nào?[^19][^15]
- Right to deletion, data portability, consent management?[^19]
- DPA (Data Processing Agreement) với data providers?

**5. Terms of Service \& Web Scraping**

- Financial sites cho phép automated access không?[^16][^15]
- Có vi phạm TOS khi scrape/automate không?
- Alternative data sources cho restricted sites?


### Method

- **Legal consultation:** Securities lawyer + fintech compliance expert[^15][^16]
- **TOS review:** Top 20 sites users visit, legal opinion on compliance[^16]
- **Privacy impact assessment:** Map data flows, identify risks[^15][^19]
- **Regulatory scan:** FCA (UK), SEC (US), EBA (EU) requirements[^16]


### Success Criteria

- Written legal opinion về investment advice positioning[^22]
- Signed data license agreements với clear usage rights
- Clear determination: KYC/AML không apply; hoặc roadmap nếu có[^15]
- GDPR/CCPA compliance plan; privacy policy reviewed[^19]
- No showstopper TOS violations; mitigation cho edge cases[^16]

***

## Phase 4: Market \& Competition – "Thị trường đủ lớn không?"

### Mục tiêu

Validate market size đủ lớn, competition không quá đông, và có sustainable differentiation.[^23][^2][^5]

### Critical Questions

**1. Market Sizing**

- Có bao nhiêu potential users? (analysts, advisors, traders)[^24][^5]
- TAM, SAM, SOM?[^24][^23]
- Market growth rate? Tailwinds?[^5]

**2. Competitive Analysis**

- Ai khác đang solve problem này?[^2][^5]
- Họ differentiate thế nào? Weakness ở đâu?[^2]
- Có agentic browser nào focus finance chưa?

**3. Switching Cost \& Adoption Barriers**

- Users đang dùng gì? (Bloomberg, FactSet, manual research)[^5]
- Switching cost cao thế nào? (workflow, data, habits)[^5]
- Friction points trong migration?[^25][^26]

**4. Competitive Moat \& Network Effects**

- Product có get better với more users không?[^5]
- Proprietary data nào build được?[^5]
- Flywheel design: more users → better X → more users?[^5]

**5. Market Timing – "Why Now?"**

- Technology changes: LLM capabilities, agentic AI mature[^27][^28]
- User behavior shifts: remote work, self-directed research[^29]
- Regulatory changes enabling/requiring new solutions?[^5]


### Method

- **Bottom-up TAM:** Count analysts tại target firms (hedge funds, RIAs, banks)[^24]
- **Top-down SAM:** Industry reports AI in finance market[^30][^31]
- **Competitive teardown:** Use competitor products, interview their users[^2]
- **SWOT analysis:** Per competitor, identify gaps[^2]
- **Trend analysis:** Technology, regulatory, user behavior[^5]


### Success Criteria

- TAM >\$500M; SAM >\$50M; SOM >\$5M trong 3 năm[^24]
- Identified 3-5 key competitors; clear differentiation articulated[^2]
- Switching cost mapped; migration plan <2hr to first value[^5]
- Network effects designed; data moat identified[^5]
- 3+ tailwinds supporting "why now" (LLM, fintech, remote, agentic AI)[^5]

***

## Phase 5: Business Model \& Monetization – "Kiếm tiền thế nào?"

### Mục tiêu

Validate pricing, unit economics, GTM, và path to profitability.[^32][^7][^5]

### Critical Questions

**1. Pricing \& Packaging**

- Users sẵn sàng pay bao nhiêu? (\$50? \$100? \$200/mo?)[^7]
- Per-seat? Per-usage? Freemium? Enterprise?[^33][^34]
- Packaging: Free/Pro/Enterprise tiers?[^7]

**2. Unit Economics**

- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) per channel?[^32][^5]
- LTV (Lifetime Value) = ARPU × retention months?[^32][^5]
- Payback period? LTV:CAC ratio?[^32][^5]
- Gross margin? Path to 60-70%?[^5]

**3. Go-to-Market Strategy**

- Làm sao acquire first 100 users?[^3][^32]
- Channels nào work? (content, community, partnerships, direct sales)[^5]
- PLG (product-led growth) vs sales-led?[^32]

**4. Retention \& Engagement**

- Aha moment là gì? Time-to-value?[^3][^7]
- Leading indicators của retention?[^7][^5]
- Monthly churn target? (aim <10%)[^5]

**5. Expansion Revenue**

- Upsell opportunities? Cross-sell?[^5]
- Usage-based pricing elements?[^5]
- NDR (Net Dollar Retention) target? (aim >110%)[^5]


### Method

- **Van Westendorp pricing:** Survey price sensitivity[^7]
- **Competitor pricing analysis:** Benchmark vs Bloomberg, FactSet, AlphaSense[^35]
- **Value-based pricing calc:** Hours saved × hourly rate = value; charge 10-20%[^5]
- **Channel experiments:** Test content, ads, community, partnerships[^5]
- **Cohort analysis:** Track retention by signup cohort[^7][^5]


### Success Criteria

- Price validated: \$50-200/mo; 60%+ would pay at target price[^7]
- LTV:CAC >3:1; payback <12 months[^32][^5]
- Identified 2-3 channels with CAC <\$500[^5]
- Aha moment defined; time-to-value <5min[^3]
- NDR target >110%; clear upsell path[^5]

***

## Phase 6: Product Strategy – "Build cái gì \& thứ tự ra sao?"

### Mục tiêu

Define MVP scope, roadmap, success metrics, và positioning.[^36][^37][^2]

### Critical Questions

**1. MVP Scope – "What's the MINIMUM?"**

- Core value = gì? 1 killer use case?[^36][^6]
- Features gì là must-have vs nice-to-have?[^37][^7]
- Kano model: Basics, Satisfiers, Delighters?[^7]
- MoSCoW: Must, Should, Could, Won't?[^37][^24]

**2. Product Roadmap – "What's next?"**

- Sau MVP, build gì tiếp theo? (V2, V3 features)[^37][^3]
- Sequencing logic? Dependencies?[^37]
- 12-month roadmap với milestones?[^3]

**3. Success Metrics \& North Star**

- North Star Metric? (ví dụ: "hours saved per user per week")[^7][^5]
- Leading indicators? (engagement, retention, NPS)[^24][^7]
- Lagging indicators? (revenue, churn, referrals)[^24]

**4. User Experience \& Onboarding**

- Aha moment xảy ra khi nào?[^3][^7]
- Time-to-first-value? (target <5min)[^3]
- Onboarding flow? Learning curve?[^36][^3]

**5. Platform vs Feature Positioning**

- Đây là platform hay feature của existing product?[^38]
- Có nguy cơ bị acquire/copy bởi Bloomberg/FactSet không?[^2]
- Path từ feature → platform nếu cần?[^5]


### Method

- **User story mapping:** Map user journey, identify critical path[^36][^3]
- **Kano model:** Survey users, categorize features[^7]
- **Opportunity solution tree:** Link features to outcomes to goals[^36][^37]
- **Prototype testing:** Lo-fi → hi-fi prototypes, measure TTV[^36][^3]
- **Now/Next/Later roadmap:** Prioritize by impact \& feasibility[^3]


### Success Criteria

- MVP scope = 1 killer use case; 5-7 core features; deliverable in 8-12 weeks[^6]
- 12-month roadmap với 3 key milestones[^3]
- North Star metric defined; 5-7 KPIs tracked[^24][^7]
- Time-to-first-value <5min; aha moment identified[^3]
- Clear positioning: finAI = "Comet for finance professionals"[^2]

***

## Phase 7: Team \& Execution – "Team gì? Build thế nào?"

### Mục tiêu

Validate có đủ team, skills, resources để execute; và timeline realistic.[^38][^2][^5]

### Critical Questions

**1. Team Composition**

- Skills cần gì? AI engineers? Finance domain experts? Full-stack?[^12][^1]
- Team size \& structure cho MVP? For scale?[^1]
- Insider (finance background) + outsider (tech) balance?[^1]

**2. Build vs Buy vs Partner**

- LLM: API (OpenAI/Anthropic) hay self-host?[^10][^11]
- Browser core: Build hay fork Chromium?[^14]
- Data: Build scrapers hay partner với providers?[^13][^11]
- Infra: AWS/GCP hay specialized platforms?[^11]

**3. Development Timeline \& Risks**

- MVP bao lâu? Beta? V1?[^3]
- Critical path? Bottlenecks?[^3]
- Top 3 risks có thể kill project?[^39]
- Mitigation plan?[^39]

**4. Funding Strategy**

- Bootstrap đến đâu? (50 users? \$10K MRR?)[^5]
- Khi nào raise? Raise bao nhiêu? (\$1-2M seed?)[^5]
- Runway sau fundraise? (target 18 months)[^5]


### Method

- **Skill gap analysis:** Current team vs needed skills[^1]
- **Build/buy framework:** Cost, time, quality, strategic value[^11]
- **Agile estimation:** Story points, sprint planning[^3]
- **Risk register:** List risks, probability, impact, mitigation[^39]
- **Financial model:** Burn rate, runway, fundraising milestones[^5]


### Success Criteria

- Team plan: 1 AI lead, 1 fintech expert, 2 full-stack, 1 designer[^1]
- Clear build/buy decisions: LLM (API), browser (Chromium), data (partner)[^11]
- Timeline: MVP 12 weeks; Beta 16 weeks; V1 24 weeks[^3]
- Top 3 risks identified với concrete mitigation[^39]
- Fundraising plan: bootstrap to \$10K MRR, then raise \$1-2M seed[^5]

## Execution Framework: Làm thế nào trong thực tế

### Week 1-2: Rapid Customer Discovery

- Line up 15 interviews với financial analysts/advisors[^1]
- 2 interviews/day × 5 days = 10 interviews week 1
- After 10-12 interviews, patterns emerge[^6][^1]
- Document pain points, current solutions, willingness-to-pay[^1]


### Week 3-4: Technical Feasibility POC

- Prompt test với real financial docs (10-Ks, earnings calls)[^10]
- Test browser automation trên top 10 sites[^14]
- Cost model: tokens × price = cost per workflow[^11]
- Security architecture first draft[^15]


### Week 5-6: Regulatory \& Legal Review

- Consult securities lawyer về investment advice positioning[^22][^15]
- Review TOS của financial sites[^16]
- Data licensing discussions với providers[^11]
- Privacy impact assessment[^19]


### Week 7-8: Business Model Validation

- Pricing survey với 50-100 potential users[^7]
- Competitive analysis \& positioning[^2]
- Unit economics model[^5]
- GTM channel experiments[^5]


### Week 9-12: MVP Definition \& Build Kickoff

- Lock MVP scope: 1 killer use case, 5-7 features[^6]
- Design prototype, test với 10 users[^36]
- Hire/contract key team members[^1]
- Sprint 0: architecture, setup, first feature[^3]


### Continuous (Parallel to above)

- Weekly user interviews – never stop[^40]
- Competitive monitoring[^2]
- Regulatory updates tracking[^15]
- Metrics dashboard setup[^7]

## Common Traps to Avoid – Lỗi PM 0→1 hay mắc

### 1. "We'll figure out monetization later"

❌ Wrong. Pricing \& business model validation = part of problem-solution fit.[^7][^5]

### 2. "Let's build everything then see if users like it"

❌ Wrong. MVP = 1 killer use case; test assumptions incrementally.[^6][^36]

### 3. "We interviewed 3 users and they loved it"

❌ Wrong. Need 12+ interviews để thấy pattern; 3 interviews = anecdotes.[^6][^1]

### 4. "Our AI is 95% accurate, that's good enough"

❌ Wrong. Finance = zero tolerance for errors; 5% error rate = trust destroyed.[^41][^42]

### 5. "We'll worry about compliance after PMF"

❌ Wrong. Compliance violations can kill company; address day 1.[^16][^15]

### 6. "If we build it, they will come"

❌ Wrong. Distribution = harder than product; validate GTM early.[^32][^5]

### 7. "Let's add one more feature before launching"

❌ Wrong. Ship fast, learn fast; every delay = competitor advantage.[^40][^6]

## Priority Matrix: What to do FIRST

### Must Do Week 1 (Go/No-Go Decisions)

1. ✅ Customer interviews (12-15) → validate pain exists[^1]
2. ✅ Technical feasibility POC → validate AI can do it[^10]
3. ✅ Legal consultation → no showstoppers[^15][^16]

### Must Do Month 1 (Build/No-Build Decisions)

4. ✅ Market sizing → TAM big enough[^24]
5. ✅ Competitive analysis → can differentiate[^2]
6. ✅ Pricing validation → users will pay[^7]
7. ✅ MVP scope lock → ready to build[^6]

### Must Do Month 2-3 (Launch/No-Launch Decisions)

8. ✅ MVP shipped to 10 beta users[^3]
9. ✅ Aha moment identified, TTV measured[^3]
10. ✅ Unit economics validated[^5]
11. ✅ GTM channel tested[^5]

***

## Final Checklist: "Are we ready to build?"

- [ ] **Problem validated:** 80%+ interviews confirm pain 8+/10[^1]
- [ ] **Solution validated:** AI accuracy 80%+, hallucination <5%[^11]
- [ ] **Market validated:** TAM >\$500M, no direct finance agentic browser competitor[^24]
- [ ] **Business model validated:** Users will pay \$50-200/mo; unit economics work[^7][^5]
- [ ] **Compliance cleared:** Legal opinion, no showstoppers[^16][^15]
- [ ] **Team ready:** AI + finance expertise covered[^1]
- [ ] **MVP scoped:** 1 killer use case, 8-12 weeks to ship[^6]

Nếu 7/7 checks = ✅ **GO.** Start building.

Nếu <5/7 checks = ⚠️ **More research needed.** Loop back.

Nếu showstopper (legal, technical, market) = ❌ **PIVOT or KILL.**

## Bảng 2: Đối Thủ Gián Tiếp

| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------ | -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| YouTube                        | Video (highlight, analysis, replay, fan-made)     | Best-in-class: auto-play 2–5 phút, trending feed, algorithm đẩy | Comment sôi nổi, like/dislike (nhưng delayed, không real-time) | Algo "for you" (nhưng generic, không sports-specific)       | 20–30 phút/session | Very High            | Curated Post-Match Hub (YouTube = infinite scroll chaotic) + Real-time emoji reaction (YouTube = delayed comments) để giữ user thêm 10–15 phút |
| Facebook Groups                | Social media (group chat, post, meme, share)      | Link từ YouTube / self-uploaded (chậm, không organized)         | Real-time react, emoji, meme, support, discussion sôi nổi      | Group-based (follow group, see group posts)                 | 10–20 phút/session | High                 | In-app emoji + poll + reply thread (real-time, official) để user chia sẻ cảm xúc trong app thay vì sang Facebook                               |
| TikTok                         | Short-form video (reaction, meme, viral clip)     | Reaction video, viral goal, short highlight (nhưng chaotic FYP) | Comment + like + share (nhưng ephemeral, không archive)        | For You FYP algo (nhưng mix sport + non-sport, khó control) | 15–25 phút/session | High                 | Organized Post-Match Hub (vs TikTok FYP chaotic) + curated content (vs TikTok algorithm push non-sports) để giữ user ở trong sports ecosystem  |

| Messenger / Telegram / Discord | Chat (group discussion, real-time)                | Chỉ share link (không video)                                    | Real-time chat, emoji, discussion (tight-knit group)           | Không personalization                                       | 10–15 phút/session | Medium (niche group) | In-app group chat (organized, official) để không phải fragmented across 5 apps khác nhau                                                       |

## 1. Executive Summary

FPT Play's Sports section faces a critical retention challenge: **7-day retention sits at 23% versus a platform average of 45%**, with users averaging only **1.2 sessions per week** against an industry benchmark of 3.5. The core problem is an **experience gap between live viewing and post-match engagement**, forcing users to fragment their journey across YouTube, social media, and third-party stats platforms.

**Key Recommendation**: Transform the Sports page from a "content directory" into a **"Personalized Sports Hub"** that captures the full emotional arc of sports fandom‚Äîbefore, during, and after matches. The MVP will focus on **Focused Fans (25‚Äì34, paid subscribers)**, delivering a **Post-Match Hub** that integrates highlights, stats, and community interaction to increase session duration by 5‚Äì10 minutes and weekly frequency to 2.0 within 6 months.

## 4. Problem Statement & Root Cause Analysis

## 4.1 The "Experience Gap"

**Problem Statement (A4 Format)**:  
_Focused Fans (25‚Äì34, paid subscribers) cannot sustain their sports passion between live matches because FPT Play lacks a post-match engagement layer, forcing them to fragment their experience across YouTube (highlights), social media (community), and third-party apps (stats). This results in 7-day retention dropping to 23% and weekly sessions stagnating at 1.2._

## 4.2 Five Whys Deep Dive

1. **Why do users exit after watching?** ‚Üí No compelling content post-match.
    
2. **Why is there no compelling content?** ‚Üí Homepage is static; no auto-surfacing of highlights, stats, or community.
    
3. **Why is content not surfaced?** ‚Üí Poor information architecture; lack of personalization engine.
    
4. **Why is personalization missing?** ‚Üí No "My Team" onboarding; metadata tagging is weak.
    
5. **Why is metadata weak?** ‚Üí Product built as "video library," not "fan hub."
    

**Root Cause**: The Sports page is designed as a **passive content repository**, not an **active engagement platform**.

## 6. Proposed Solution Framework

## 6.1 Vision: "The Sports Hub That Lives With You"

Transform FPT Play Sports from a **video directory** into a **personalized, interactive fan hub** that captures the full emotional arc: anticipation ‚Üí live action ‚Üí celebration/commiseration ‚Üí ongoing connection.

## 6.2 Core Concept: Post-Match Hub + My Team Feed

**Concept 1: Post-Match Hub (MBP Priority #1)**  
_Auto-activates when live match ends._

- **Auto-play Highlight**: 60-second recap with BLV voiceover.
    
- **Match Stats**: Heatmaps, possession, key player performance.
    
- **BLV Recap**: 1‚Äì2 minute video analysis (exclusive to FPT).
    
- **Emoji Reaction Bar**: üî• üòÇ üíî (real-time sentiment).
    
- **Poll**: "MVP of the match?" ‚Üí results live.
    
- **Related Content**: Links to full replay, upcoming fixtures, team news.
    

**Concept 2: My Team Feed (MBP Priority #2)**  
_Onboarding: "Pick your team(s)" ‚Üí personalized homepage._

- **Custom Feed**: Only content (highlights, news, stats) for selected teams/leagues.
    
- **Match Alerts**: Pre-match, lineups, kick-off, goals.
    
- **Team Stats Dashboard**: Season performance, player rankings.
    
- **Fan Community**: Moderated chat/comments with fellow supporters.
    

**Concept 3: In-Match Interaction Layer (Quick Win)**

- Emoji reactions during live stream.
    
- Quick polls: "Should they substitute now?"
    
- Live chat (opt-in, friends-only or team fan club).

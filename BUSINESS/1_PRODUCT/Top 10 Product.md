1. **Director/Head of Product** - Highest level (Tue Ngo, Thuc Binh Lam, Georgina Pang)

4. **Product Lead/Executive** - Mid-senior level (Phượng Trần, Nhi Dang)
    
5. **Product Owner** - Mid level (Duy Long Lương)
